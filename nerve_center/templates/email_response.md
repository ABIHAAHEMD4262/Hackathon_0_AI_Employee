# Email Response Template

## Standard Reply

```
Hi {{recipient_name}},

Thank you for your email.

{{body}}

Best regards,
{{sender_name}}
{{sender_title}}
{{company_name}}
```

## Meeting Request Reply

```
Hi {{recipient_name}},

Thank you for reaching out about scheduling a meeting.

I'd be happy to connect. Here are some available times:
{{available_slots}}

Alternatively, feel free to book directly: {{calendly_link}}

Looking forward to speaking with you!

Best,
{{sender_name}}
```

## Invoice Acknowledgment

```
Hi {{recipient_name}},

Thank you for sending the invoice #{{invoice_number}}.

I've received it and logged it for processing. The amount of {{amount}} with due date {{due_date}} has been noted.

If you have any questions, please let me know.

Best,
{{sender_name}}
```

## Out of Office

```
Thank you for your email.

I'm currently unavailable and will respond to your message when I return on {{return_date}}.

For urgent matters, please contact {{emergency_contact}}.

Best regards,
{{sender_name}}
```

## Follow-up

```
Hi {{recipient_name}},

I wanted to follow up on my previous email regarding {{subject}}.

{{followup_body}}

Please let me know if you have any questions or need additional information.

Best,
{{sender_name}}
```

## Decline (Polite)

```
Hi {{recipient_name}},

Thank you for thinking of me for {{opportunity}}.

After careful consideration, I've decided to pass on this opportunity at this time. {{reason_if_appropriate}}

I appreciate you reaching out and wish you the best.

Best regards,
{{sender_name}}
```
