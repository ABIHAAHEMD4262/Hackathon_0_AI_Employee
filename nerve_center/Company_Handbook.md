### Email Triage Rules

|-------------------|----------|--------|

| "meeting", "schedule", "call" | Calendar | Check availability, draft response |

## Automation Boundaries

### AI CAN Autonomously:
- [x] Read and categorize emails
- [x] Draft responses (held for review if configured)
- [x] Update Dashboard and logs
- [x] Schedule reminders
- [x] Extract data from documents
- [x] Organize files into appropriate folders
- [x] Send pre-approved template responses

### AI MUST Ask Before:
- [ ] Sending any email (unless pre-approved template)
- [ ] Making any purchase
- [ ] Scheduling meetings with new contacts
- [ ] Sharing any confidential information
- [ ] Responding to legal or HR matters
- [ ] Any action involving money

### AI Should NEVER:
- Access banking systems directly
- Share passwords or security codes
- Respond to phishing attempts
- Make commitments on your behalf without approval
- Delete important files without backup
- Contact emergency services (except true emergencies)
